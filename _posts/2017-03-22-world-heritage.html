中国有50处世界遗产，数量为世界第二，世界遗产监测预警系统是一套用于保护遗产地以及文物的先进平台，是我在2013～2015年间参与的主要项目，该系统结合了物联网和Web平台，主要对中国的世界遗产地进行实时监测，我负责了所有的前端开发工作，我参与期间，该系统已经在周口店、长城、龙门石窟、天地之中等多个重要的世界遗产地部署，项目总额近2000万。

China has 50 World Heritage Sites, ranking second in the world, this system is designed to monitor World Heritage Site in real time, it is the main project I participated during 2013~2015, I was responsible for all the Front-End development work, before I had left, this system had been deployed in multiple famous World Heritage Site, including "Peking Man Site at Zhoukoudian", "The Great Wall", "Longmen Grottoes", "The Centre of Heaven and Earth".

前端技术栈：Jquery、RequireJS、HighCharts、OpenLayers、GoogleMap、Less等，后端：ASP.NET、SQLServer。

Front-end technology stack: Jquery, RequireJS, HighCharts, OpenLayers, GoogleMap, Less, etc., back-end: ASP.NET, SQLServer and so on.
