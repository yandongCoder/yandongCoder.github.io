这套平台是我基于世界遗产监测预警项目中的积累，是一套概念先进的物联网监测平台，软件由我自己开发而成，硬件基于西班牙的Liblium系列产品。

This platform was based on the accumulation of previous projects, the software was developed by myself, and the hardware was besed on Liblium.

根据之前项目的积累，我提出了这个架构。

According to the accumulation of previous projects, I made this framework.


每个传感器产品都内涵一个GPS芯片，通过Wifi或3G传输，将位置和最后一个数据显示在地图上

Each sensor has a GPS chip, through the Wifi or 3G, the location and the last data will displayed on the map
