虽然我是一名前端工程师，但我在大多数项目中同时兼顾着设计工作（使用Photoshop），我一直认为前端和设计密不可分，一个前端只有很好的掌握了设计的基础，才能用最高效率，在最大程度上实现一个兼顾页面优雅和代码优雅的作品。

Although I am a front-end engineer, but in most of my projects, I am also a designer(using Photoshop), I believe that front-end development and design are inseparable, a front-end engineer needs to master some basic design concept and skill, so as to write elegant code and also achieve an elegant interface.
